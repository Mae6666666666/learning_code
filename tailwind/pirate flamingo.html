Vinsmoke Sanji

Woman Radar:

Sanji has an amazing ability to sense any pretty lady within a large radius. He uses this ability to try and charm whoever that lucky lady is and frequently gets nosebleeds or turns to stone from how pretty they are to him.


Diable Jambe:

With this, Sanji can essentially turn his leg on fire, delivering a painful and powerful kick to whoever he sends it to.


Iconic Quote:

"When a woman lies.... a real man forgives her"
