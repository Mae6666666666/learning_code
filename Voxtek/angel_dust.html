Angel Dust. The ultimate star in hell. Anthony is a sinner in hell with his appearance based off a spider due to his "web of crime" when he was alive. In the beginning, he shows no change or desire to be redeemed at the Hazbin Hotel, however, more in season 2, he shows significant improvements, even getting a comment from Husk, mentioning "the only person down here to be redeemed is Pentious, and Angel on a good day". He has great character development, and I got so surprised when he was revealed to be a spy (not by choice). I can't wait to see more of him in season 3!
