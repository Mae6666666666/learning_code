Velvette! The backbone of the Vees! This sassy, confident doll demon has a love for social media, fashion, and fame in a way. She is the only lady of the Vees, and primarily works in the fashion industry. She is one of the most emotionally stable and level headed out of the three main antagonists, showing no sympathy for the change in Vox's god complex personality, grounding Valentino, not letting Vox have an excuse to alienate the rest of the Vees.
